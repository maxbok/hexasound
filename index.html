Description

HexASound turns your iPad, iPhone, and iPod touch into a multi-tone generator instrument. The original keyboard layout, based on hexagons, has been especially optimized for multi-touch devices. It allows you to easily play any kind of melodies or chords in a bounded screen.

For beginners, learning music would never be that ergonomic. This geometrical layout is a really intuitive representation of chords and scales. It will make you start in no time!

If you are a seasoned musician, HexASound can be used as an instrument in its own right or as a tool to help you transpose your favorite patterns.

    HexASound highlights
  • Easy transposition
  • Scalable keyboard layout
  • Many sound filters
  • Sound customization while playing
